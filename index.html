Aurora Salon and Spa consists of a group of friends who love to create hairstyles that are wearable, fashionable and that bring out the unique beauty in each of our guests. We admire each other for our personal interests and strengths, and we came together to create a warm and relaxed salon environment. We laugh a lot. We love to learn from each other and call Limegrove home. Aurora Salon and Spa was designed to feel inclusive, to make new friends, and to convey the feeling of visiting a friend’s home.

Our mission here at Aurora Salon and Spa is: "To treat every guest in the salon with professional care and respect."

Feel free to peruse our site and get to know us. We promise to be the salon you love to visit, and the stylists you trust to provide the look that captures your best self. We look forward to meeting you.
